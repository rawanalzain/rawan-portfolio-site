About Rawan

Bridging the worlds of creative expression and social impact.

Rawan Alzain is a Dubai-based presenter, DJ, and cultural facilitator whose work spans radio, live events, and voice-over. Active since 2016, she designs audience-centered formats that amplify emerging talent and cross-cultural dialogue.

Recognized as Sudan's first female DJ and a trained medical doctor, Rawan brings a disciplined, multifaceted perspective to the media landscape. Her contributions have been recognized by the Clinton Global Initiative (NYC) and Art, Design & Audio Connect (Abu Dhabi).
